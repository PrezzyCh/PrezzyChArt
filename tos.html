Ordering Commissions

Limit of one commission per person per slot opening. I have the right to refuse or drop the commission for the circumstances listed in the TOS, external issues, or issues related to the commission. Your commission will be rejected if there are no slots available as status updates on social media regarding slot availability may not be accurate.

You agree to the terms upon ordering a commission from me.

Ordering Commissions

Payment will be made with Paypal and must be made upfront within 3 days once the commission is next in the queue and any fees after the completion of the artwork.
Upon refund, the artwork may be re-purposed for another artwork.

  • Before any activity done - 100%

  • Sketching - 90%

  • Lineart - 70%

  • Detailing/Rendering - 50%

  • Lighting & FX - 30%

  • Completed - 0%

Full refund is given if im unable to complete the commission.

Communication and Edits

Communication will be done on the predetermined set of platforms of your choosing. An initial sketch will be sent before continuing with the commission. Updates will continue on each stage of the commission (ie lineart, texturing, shading, finals) or upon your request. I will not accept any file format or links as reference except for png and jpeg that will be shown on embeds.

You may request edits during the commission but large edits may accrued small edit fees that must be paid at the conclusion of the commission.

Delivery time and Queue

Turnaround time may vary depending on the complexity of the commission and depending on queue length. Estimated delivery time excluding the queue is 2 weeks.

Commissions will be done on a first come first serve basis and will be put into a queue. You will be notified when your commission is next in line and payment must be made in 3 days or the commission will be dropped. Subsequent applications will be put at the end of the queue.
Please do not pay until you are next in the queue!

As I am not a full time artist please be patient as commissions may take time. Thx! ♥️

Artwork Completion

Once the artwork is completed, any accrued fees must be paid before the non-watermarked version will be sent to your email. Edits after payment will not be accepted.

Completed art may be used in my gallery and/or put on my socials unless requested to be private.

Usage and Copyright

The art shall only be used in non-commercial purposes (ie profile pictures, wallpapers, or personal prints) and can be shared on your socials.

The artwork is not created by you and you may not claim credit of its creation. When sharing on socials, you must provide visible credit and a link on one of the sites listed below.
